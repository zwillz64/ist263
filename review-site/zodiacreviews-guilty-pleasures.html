Guilty Pleasures

For the films that in normal critical context are probably not good, but resonated with me so much either due to nostalgia or favorite tropes that I still love them as an adult.
Don't expect a lot with these, but try to have a good time.

  • Big Fat Liar (2002)

    PG, 88 mins. Directed by Shawn Levy.
    Starring Frankie Muniz, Amanda Bynes, Paul Giamatti


  • Shark Tale (2004)

    PG, 90 mins. Directed by Bibo Bergeron, Vicky Jenson, & Rob Letterman
    Starring Will Smith, Robert De Niro, Jack Black


  • The Mummy Returns (2001)

    PG-13, 130 mins. Directed by Stephen Sommers.
    Starring Brendan Fraser, Rachel Weisz, John Hannah


  • Max Keeble's Big Move (2001)

    PG, 86 mins. Directed by Tim Hill.
    Starring Alex D. Linz, Larry Miller, Jamie Kennedy


  • Transformers (2007)

    PG-13, 144 mins. Directed by Michael Bay.
    Starring Shia LaBeouf, Megan Fox, Josh Duhamel


  • Vantage Point (2008)

    PG-13, 90 mins. Directed by Pete Travis.
    Starring Dennis Quaid, Forest Whitaker, William Hurt


  • Home Alone 3 (1997)

    PG-13, 102 mins. Directed by Raja Gosnell.
    Starring Alex D. Linz, Olek Krupa, Rya Kihlstedt


  • Space Jam (1996)

    PG, 88 mins. Directed by Joe Pytka.
    Starring Michael Jordan, Bill Murray, Wayne Knight
