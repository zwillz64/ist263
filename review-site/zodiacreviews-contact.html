Zodiac: A Brief Biography

Humble Beginnings

Zodiac was born in New York City. He lived with his sister and his parents for several years. He always had an eye for film, becoming interested in the workings of movie productions before he turned 5. He became obsessed with watching as many films as he could and studied movie trivia to near photographic memory.

Recent Years

After attending Syracuse University for 5 years studying computer and information sciences, Zodiac currently resides in Syracuse. When he’s not watching movies and playing video games, you can probably find Zodiac… sleeping.
