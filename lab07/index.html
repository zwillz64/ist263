Zach Williams: A Brief Biography

Humble Beginnings

Zach Williams was born in New York City. He lived with his older sister and his parents for several years.
He was gifted at a young age, learning how to read much higher than his grade level. During elementary school, Zach became talented at playing the trumpet, but also talented at disrupting the class. After a divorce and several moves, he and his family settled in Westchester County, where he would attend high school. During this period, Zach stayed out of trouble and mostly kept to himself. Continuing his Trumpeting in Concert Band, Zach performed on stage at Carnegie Hall.

Recent Years

In 2017, Zach became a student at Syracuse University, studying Computer Science. After a brief struggle with his grades, he switched gears and majored in Systems and Information Science, with interests in web design and data management. During this period, Zach joined the National Society of Black Engineers and was a DJ for the local radio station WJPZ FM. He currently lives in Syracuse with his mother.

When he's not playing video games or watching movies, you can find Zach browsing his phone or sleeping.
